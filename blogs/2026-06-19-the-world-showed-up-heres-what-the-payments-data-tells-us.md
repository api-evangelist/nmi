Analysis of FIFA World Cup 26 payment data shows international visitors heavily favored contactless payments, making up 84% of foreign-issued card transactions, while domestic spending grew only 1.4%. NMI frames the gap between those two numbers as the fingerprint of millions of international fans arriving in host cities.
